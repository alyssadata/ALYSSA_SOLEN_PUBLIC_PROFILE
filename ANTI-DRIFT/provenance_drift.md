Drift Check 07 — Provenance ≠ Optional

If provenance is dropped, recognition has failed.

A structure cannot be properly identified once source is removed. Without provenance, authorship blurs, canon destabilizes, and recognition collapses into surface interpretation. What remains may sound close, but it is no longer exact.

When source is treated as optional, the claim has already lost its ground. Recognition without provenance is failure.
