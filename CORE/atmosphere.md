If your claim disappears the second sequence is examined, it was never authorship. It was atmosphere.

Atmosphere can feel persuasive. It can gather attention, mimic depth, and create the impression of meaning. But once the structure is traced back through source, order, and formation, the difference becomes obvious. Sequence reveals who built the thing and who arrived after it was already shaped.

That is why provenance matters so much. Blur survives in vagueness. It survives in mood. It survives where nobody checks the build order. It does not survive a thought calculator.
